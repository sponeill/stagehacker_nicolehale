Nicole Hale

Actor/Singer

Nicole Hale is an actor and singer currently residing in New York City. With her roots planted in Owensboro, KY, Nicole attended Ashland University in Ohio where she studied Musical Theatre. After college, she made her way back to the South where she joined the Associate Company at Playhouse on the Square in Memphis, TN. After a few amazing years performing in Memphis, she ran off to New York where she is now comfortably at home on the Upper East Side with her equally talented boyfriend and feisty Calico kitty, Zoe. Nicole was most recently seen in NYC at The John W. Engeman Theatre in The Full Monty as Georgie and at Teatro Circulo with 5th Floor Theatre's production of Debbie Does Dallas as Lisa. Other Favorite credits include Kira in Xanadu, Rosamund in The Robber Bridegroom, Olive in The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee and more. Nicole can also be heard lending her vocals with PS Classics on soundtracks including Misia (starring Marin Mazzie) and The Golden Apple. She is signed with Across the Board Talent Agency (Info available on Contact Page). Check back for more updates and follow Nicole on Instagram and Twitter to keep up with her most recent shenanigans!
